HIT TRACK PRO - APP STORE DESCRIPTION
========================================

Track, analyze, and scout opponent teams with Hit Track Pro – the ultimate hit tracking app built specifically for softball and baseball coaches.

POWERFUL HIT TRACKING
• Record every hit with location, type, and pitch details
• Tap the field to mark exact hit locations
• Track singles, doubles, triples, home runs, and outs
• Color-coded spray charts show hitting patterns at a glance

SCOUT MULTIPLE OPPONENTS
• Track unlimited opponent teams
• Each team has its own player roster
• Organize players by lineup order
• Quick team switching during games

DETAILED ANALYTICS
• Visual spray charts show where hitters send the ball
• Hit type breakdown (singles, doubles, triples, HRs, outs)
• Pitch location analysis (inside, outside, up, down, middle)
• Filter stats by individual player or entire team
• Date range filtering to focus on recent games

PROFESSIONAL REPORTS
• Export beautiful PDF reports with your team logo
• Share via email, Messages, AirDrop, or save to Files
• Perfect for sharing with players, parents, or fellow coaches
• Date range included in exports

UNIVERSAL APP - IPHONE & IPAD
• Native support for iPhone and iPad
• Optimized layouts for all screen sizes
• Seamlessly sync data across all your Apple devices
• Access your scouting reports anywhere - iPhone, iPad, or both
• Automatic backup to iCloud keeps your data safe
• Works offline – syncs automatically when connected

BUILT FOR COACHES
• Clean, intuitive interface designed for quick entry during games
• Dark mode support for evening games
• No learning curve – start tracking in seconds
• Completely free – no subscriptions, no in-app purchases
• Privacy-focused – your data stays on your devices

PERFECT FOR ALL LEVELS
Whether you're coaching Little League, high school, travel ball, or competitive softball, Hit Track Pro gives you the insights you need to develop better game strategies and scout opponents effectively.
