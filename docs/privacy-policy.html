Privacy Policy for Hit Track Pro

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Hit Track Pro ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information Collection and Use

Data We DO NOT Collect

Hit Track Pro does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information with us or third parties. Specifically:

  • No personal identification information
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or third-party data sharing
  • No account creation or login required
  • No contact information collected
  • No location data collected

Data You Create

All data you enter into Hit Track Pro is stored locally on your device and/or in your personal iCloud account:

  • Opponent team names
  • Player information (names, numbers)
  • Hit tracking data (locations, types, pitch details)
  • Team logos (photos you upload)
  • App settings and preferences

This data belongs to you and never leaves your control.

iCloud Sync

Hit Track Pro uses Apple's iCloud to sync your data across your devices signed into the same iCloud account. This feature:

  • Is optional (requires iCloud to be enabled on your device)
  • Uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure
  • Syncs data only between devices signed into YOUR iCloud account
  • Is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Does not share your data with us or any third parties

We have no access to your iCloud data. All syncing is handled directly by Apple's iCloud service.

Photo Library Access

When you upload a team logo, Hit Track Pro requests permission to access your Photo Library. This permission:

  • Is only used to select a photo for your team logo
  • Does not upload photos to any server
  • Stores the selected photo locally on your device
  • Can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings

Data Storage

All app data is stored in two locations under your control:

  1. Local Storage: UserDefaults on your device in the app's sandboxed container
  2. iCloud: Key-Value Store in your personal iCloud account

Neither location is accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Sharing

Hit Track Pro allows you to export PDF reports containing your statistics. When you use the export feature:

  • You choose who to share the PDF with (via Messages, Email, AirDrop, etc.)
  • The PDF contains only the statistics you choose to export
  • No data is sent to us or any third party servers
  • All sharing is done directly through iOS sharing capabilities

Children's Privacy

Hit Track Pro does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed for coaches to track opponent statistics and requires no personal information.

Third-Party Services

Hit Track Pro does NOT use:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Third-party data processors
  • External servers or APIs

The only external service used is Apple's iCloud (optional), which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Data Security

Your data security is important:

  • All data is stored in iOS's secure app sandbox
  • iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • You control all data deletion through the app's reset features

Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Export: Generate PDF reports of your statistics
  • Delete: Reset team data or all data via Settings
  • Control: Disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings if desired

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in:

  • This document (with updated "Last Updated" date)
  • App Store listing updates
  • In-app notifications for significant changes
