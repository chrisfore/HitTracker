⚾ Hit Track Pro

Softball & Baseball Hit Tracking for Coaches

📱 Universal App - iPhone & iPad

Scout Opponents, Win More Games

The ultimate opponent hit tracking app built specifically for softball and baseball coaches. Record hit locations, analyze spray charts, and develop winning game strategies. Runs natively on both iPhone and iPad.

📍 Hit Location Tracking

Tap the field to record exact hit locations. Visual spray charts show where each batter hits the ball.

👥 Multiple Opponent Teams

Track unlimited opponent teams throughout the season, each with their own player roster.

📊 Detailed Analytics

Hit type breakdown, pitch location analysis, and spray charts for every player and team.

📤 PDF Reports

Export professional scouting reports with your team logo and share with coaching staff.

🤝 Data Sharing

Share scouting data with other coaches via .hitdata files. Import received data with one tap.

☁️ iCloud Sync

Seamlessly sync data across iPhone and iPad with automatic backup to iCloud.

📱 Universal App

Native support for iPhone and iPad. One purchase, works on both devices.

🔒 Privacy First

No data collection, no tracking, no ads. Your scouting data stays on your devices.
