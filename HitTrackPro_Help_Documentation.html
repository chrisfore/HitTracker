📋 Overview

Hit Track Pro helps softball and baseball coaches track and analyze where opponent batters hit the ball during games. Record hit locations, pitch information, and view spray charts to identify hitting patterns for multiple opponent teams.

👥 Setting Up Teams

When you first open the app, you'll set up your first opponent team:

  • Enter the Team Name (your opponent's name)
  • Add Players with their number (name is optional)
  • Players appear in lineup order (you can reorder later)
  • Tap Start Tracking when ready

You can add more opponent teams later in Settings.

⚾ Tracking Hits

On the Track screen:

  1. Select the opponent team using the dropdown at the top
  2. Select the player using the player dropdown
  3. Tap on the field where the ball was hit
  4. Choose the hit type:
    • Single - safe on first base
    • Double - safe on second base
    • Triple - safe on third base
    • HR - home run
    • Groundout - out on ground ball
    • Flyout - out on fly ball
    • Error - reached base on error
  5. Optionally add pitch information:
    • Pitch Location: Inside, Outside, Up, Down, Middle
  6. Tap Save to record the hit

👁️ Understanding the Display

When a player is selected, you'll see:

  • Spray Chart - visual field display showing all hit locations
  • Colored dots on the field showing previous hit locations:
    • Green = Single
    • Blue = Double
    • Yellow = Triple
    • Red = Home Run
    • Gray = Outs
    • Orange = Error
  • Pitch stats bar - tap to filter hits by pitch location
  • Legend showing hit type colors

📊 Viewing Results

The Results tab shows detailed statistics:

  • Team filter - select which opponent team to view
  • Player filter - view individual player stats or team overview
  • Date filtering - filter stats by date range
  • Spray chart - visual hit distribution
  • Hit type breakdown - counts by type (singles, doubles, etc.)
  • Pitch patterns - where hits occurred (inside, outside, etc.)
  • PDF Export - share professional scouting reports

⚙️ Managing Teams

In Settings, you can:

  • Switch teams - select from the team picker
  • Create new teams - add more opponent teams to track
  • Change team name - tap to edit
  • Delete teams - remove a team and all its data
  • Add players - tap Add Player (number required, name optional)
  • Reorder lineup - drag players using the handle
  • Remove players - swipe left to delete
  • Clear player hits - remove one player's data
  • Clear all hits - reset all hit data for the team
  • Add team logo - customize with your own logo
  • Dark Mode - toggle for evening games

☁️ iCloud Sync

Your data automatically syncs across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account:

  • All opponent teams, players, and hit data sync automatically
  • Works offline - syncs when connection is restored
  • Your data is private and never leaves your iCloud account
  • Enable iCloud in iOS Settings if sync isn't working

📤 Exporting Reports

Create professional PDF scouting reports:

  • Go to Results tab
  • Select team and optionally a player
  • Use date filter if needed
  • Tap Export PDF
  • Share via email, Messages, AirDrop, or save to Files
  • Reports include spray charts, hit breakdowns, and your team logo

💡 Tips & Best Practices

  • Track pitch location to identify patterns for each batter
  • Use spray charts to determine defensive positioning
  • Review opponent data before games for strategic planning
  • Export PDFs to share scouting reports with your coaching staff
  • Use Dark Mode for better visibility at evening games
  • The lineup order matches batting order - reorder as needed
  • Add multiple opponent teams to track throughout the season
