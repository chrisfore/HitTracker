WHAT'S NEW IN VERSION 1.15

SHARE SCOUTING DATA WITH OTHER COACHES
• Share individual player data, entire teams, or all your data with fellow coaches
• Export as .hitdata files via AirDrop, Messages, email, or any sharing method
• Import data from other coaches — just tap a .hitdata file or use the Import button in Settings
• Imported data is added alongside your existing data with no conflicts

IMPROVED iPAD EXPERIENCE
• Redesigned hit input layout for better button visibility on iPad
• Larger spray charts for improved readability on bigger screens
• Optimized layouts for all screen sizes

ADJUSTABLE TEXT SIZE
• New text size controls in Settings — choose from System, Small, Medium, Large, or Extra Large
• Applied app-wide for a comfortable viewing experience

PDF SCOUTING REPORTS
• Export professional PDF reports with spray charts and hit breakdowns
• Include your team logo on reports
• Filter by date range to focus on recent games
• Share reports with players, parents, or coaching staff

ADDITIONAL IMPROVEMENTS
• Dark mode support for evening games
• iCloud sync keeps your data up to date across all your Apple devices
• Completely free — no subscriptions, no ads
